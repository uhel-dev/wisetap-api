Data Deletion Instructions

WiseTap is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your information. Since our service does not collect or store any personal information through our API, there is no personal data to delete. However, we understand the importance of data control, and we provide you with the following information on how you can manage or delete your data directly through Facebook:

Managing Your Facebook Data

If you wish to manage or delete your data on Facebook, you can do so by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to the Settings & Privacy section.
  3. Click on Your Facebook Information.
  4. From here, you can view, download, or delete your information.

For more detailed instructions, please visit the Facebook Help Center.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding data management, please feel free to contact us at support@wisetap.co.uk.
