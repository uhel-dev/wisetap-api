Privacy Policy

At WiseTap, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the handling of your information and ensures our commitment to protecting your privacy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal data such as Facebook usernames or any other personal information through our API. The API is designed solely to facilitate users in finding their own profiles for the purpose of ordering WiseTap products without retaining any personal information.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, no data is used for any purpose outside the scope of our business. The API functions merely to enhance user convenience in associating Facebook profiles with WiseTap orders.

Your Rights and Choices

As we do not collect or store any personal data, there are no direct rights or choices that need to be managed. Should you wish to manage your information on Facebook, please refer to the Facebook settings and privacy policy.

Data Deletion

To reiterate, WiseTap does not store any personal data that could be deleted. If you have concerns about your data on Facebook, you can manage or delete your information by following the instructions available in your Facebook account settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@wisetap.co.uk.
